# QuickCalc Tools - Free Online Calculators

## WHAT WE DO
We provide free, instant calculators for everyday decisions.
No signup, no downloads, no BS - just fast, accurate calculations.

## WHO USES THIS
People who need quick answers for:
- Financial decisions (loans, discounts, tips)
- Health tracking (BMI, pregnancy)
- Daily life (bill splitting, unit conversions, age)

## AVAILABLE TOOLS
Tip Calculator - Calculate tips and split bills
Loan Calculator - Monthly payments and interest
Pregnancy Calculator - Due dates and tracking
BMI Calculator - Body mass index and calories
Discount Calculator - Sale prices and savings
Age Calculator - Exact age calculations
Split Bill Calculator - Divide costs fairly
Unit Converter - Measurement conversions

## WHY WEB CALCULATORS?
✓ No app download required
